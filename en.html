About me

My name is Alice. I’m a frontend developer and before that web-designer, digital illustrator, based in Saint-Petersburg.

I have a big passion for art and design, specifically how these disciplines can integrate with modern web technology. For this reason I decided to learn HTML/CSS/JS and I really like it.

Using the pre-processing technology Less, architectural methodology - BEM, React.js, JavaScript (ES6), I try to produce clean, modular code.

I have over 5 years in graphic design experience. In free time I'm illustrator. I have very long usage experience Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop. By using Wacom tablet and Adobe applications combined with traditional illustration I deliver compelling digital illustration.
